origin

A Timeline of Rock Paper Scissors

Life appears on earth

Roughly 3.7 billion years ago, shortly after the formation of Earth, life emerges in the ocean. Single-cell organisms were indeed found in rocks from western Greenland.

Life takes a step on land

The first Earth walk, accomplished by a millipede-like creature in Scottland dates back 425 million years, according to recent discoveries. Trees did not exist yet.

The first humans evolve into being

The first human ancestors appeared between 5,000,000 and 7,000,000 years ago in Africa. They walked on two legs, freeing their front limbs for the greatest invention: Rock Paper Scissors.

Humans invent Rock Paper Scissors

In modern-day Somalia, some 1,500,000 years ago, humans develop a revolutionary way of settling situations whose outcomes seem uncertain. They will change the course of history.

Humans tame fire

The oldest evidence of taming fire was found in Israel's Qesem Cave and was placed 400,000 years ago.
